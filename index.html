ABOUT US

Yacht.Camp is the best place for sailing

Yacht.Camp is a perfect place where everyone can test their skills, improve sail techniques, or simply enjoy time among fellow sailors. Our mission is to spread the idea that the most eco-friendly way to travel is by sail.

By offering comprehensive training in sailing, we are not only creating skilled sailors but also advocates for a sustainable and responsible approach to travel and ocean conservation.

Contact Us

Our Principles

At Yacht.Camp, we believe that sailing is not just a skill but a way to connect with nature and embrace a sustainable lifestyle. Our commitment to education stems from several key principles:

  • 01

    Empowerment Through Knowledge

    We equip individuals with the skills to navigate confidently, fostering independence and a unique perspective on the world.

  • 02

    Sustainability

    Sailing is an eco-friendly travel option, promoting alternatives to fossil fuel-dependent methods.

  • 03

    Community Building

    Our camps unite individuals from diverse backgrounds who share a passion for the sea, fostering lasting friendships through teamwork.

  • 04

    Personal Development

    Sailing challenges participants physically and mentally, enhancing problem-solving, resilience, and adaptability.

  • 05

    Respect for the Ocean

    We emphasize ocean conservation, teaching environmental stewardship and the impact of our activities on marine ecosystems.
