ABOUT US

Yacht.Camp is the best place for sailing

Yacht.Camp is a perfect place where everyone can test their skills, improve sail techniques, or simply enjoy time among fellow sailors. Our mission is to spread the idea that the most eco-friendly way to travel is by sail.

By offering comprehensive training in sailing, we are not only creating skilled sailors but also advocates for a sustainable and responsible approach to travel and ocean conservation.

Contact Us

Our Principles

At Yacht.Camp, we believe that sailing is not just a skill but a way to connect with nature and embrace a sustainable lifestyle. Our commitment to education stems from several key principles:

  • 01

    Empowerment Through Knowledge

    We equip individuals with the skills to navigate confidently, fostering independence and a unique perspective on the world.

  • 02

    Sustainability

    Sailing is an eco-friendly travel option, promoting alternatives to fossil fuel-dependent methods.

  • 03

    Community Building

    Our camps unite individuals from diverse backgrounds who share a passion for the sea, fostering lasting friendships through teamwork.

  • 04

    Personal Development

    Sailing challenges participants physically and mentally, enhancing problem-solving, resilience, and adaptability.

  • 05

    Respect for the Ocean

    We emphasize ocean conservation, teaching environmental stewardship and the impact of our activities on marine ecosystems.

 Our Instructors

IGOR AKIMENKO

Leading sailing coach, master of sports and winner of international regattas, Igor is known for his perfect command of the “language of the winds”.

With over 20 years of teaching experience, he is the most experienced instructor on our team. He holds the Sailing Coach certification from the National University of Physical Education and Sports, IYT, ICC (Spanish version) and the KSSF of Ukraine certificate.

His knowledge and experience allow him to prepare athletes for high achievements and develop an intuitive understanding of sea conditions. The captain is guided by the principle: “A motivated person always succeeds”, helping students to unleash their potential and reach new heights in sports and beyond.

MIKHAIL KONSTANTAS

With more than 10 years of experience in yachting and sailing, Mikhail is a master of leadership who creates an atmosphere of trust and cooperation.

Certified according to ISSA and Ukrainian Sailing Federation standards, he speaks English, Greek, Ukrainian, Russian and French, which facilitates effective work with international crews.

His leadership skills and understanding of psychology allow him to unite the team, identify everyone's strengths and inspire joint achievements. Thanks to a clear distribution of roles and tasks, the crew demonstrates cohesion, readiness for challenges and commitment to principle: “A strong team can overcome anything.”

Viktor Kharchevskyi

With over 13 years of experience in yachting and sailing, Viktor holds IYT, ISSA and Ukrainian Sailing Federation certifications. He has knowledge of key techniques such as tacking, jibing and sail setting, paying attention to important details.

By teaching safety and confidence, he creates a comfortable environment for practising skills such as manoeuvres with a victim overboard and chart navigation. His lessons also cover wind conditions and currents.

He is fluent in English, Ukrainian and Russian. His principle: ‘Any adventure that life gives us, we can overcome’, inspires his students to become confident sailors.
