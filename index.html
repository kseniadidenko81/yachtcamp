Why Our Yachting Course?

Why Our Yachting Course?

Sail the Atlantic, Feel the Freedom

Tenerife offers ideal sailing conditions year-round, with its mix of calm seas and exhilarating winds. You won’t just learn to sail—you’ll experience the magic of yachting on some of the most beautiful waters in the world.

Personalized Instruction

Whether you're a complete beginner or already have some experience, our expert instructors tailor the course to your level, ensuring you get the most out of every moment. Learn at your own pace, with plenty of one-on-one attention.

Fast-Track to Competency

In just two days, you'll gain the essential knowledge and skills you need to confidently sail your own boat. We'll teach you everything from rigging, navigation, and safety, to advanced techniques like reading the wind and plotting your course.

Experience & LEARN

This journey is designed to give you real-world practice, refining your skills in steering, navigation, and handling the yacht in varying Atlantic conditions near Canaria islands.

  • Duration: 1 day - 1 night - 1 day (starting at 10:00 and finishing by 18:00 the next day)
  • Overnight Stay: Experience the tranquility of anchoring near the island, spending the night on board, surrounded by the beauty of the Atlantic.
  • Wildlife Encounters: Keep an eye out for dolphins and whales, that frequent the waters around the Canary Islands, adding an extra layer of excitement and wonder to your adventure.

This course focuses on practical, hands-on sailing. This experience builds the competence and confidence to manage a sailboat in real conditions.

  • Sail 100-150 nM: You’ll cover significant distances, gaining essential navigational skills as you sail between the islands.
  • Master Boat Handling: Practice the art of tacking, jibing, and steering a yacht in open waters.
  • Night Sailing Experience: Learn the nuances of sailing after dark, adding a new layer to your skillset as you navigate by stars and instruments.
