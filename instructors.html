Viktor Kharchevsky

An insightful Methodologist who captures the essence of sailing, focusing on the details that matter. With years of experience on the water, he knows key sailing techniques like tacking, jibing, and sail trim.

He teaches safety and builds confidence, creating a friendly space for students to practice skills like man-overboard drills and navigation with charts. His lessons cover important topics like wind patterns and currents, helping learners understand sailing better.
